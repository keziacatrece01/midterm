Welcome to Z's Candles. Where we house the scents of success!

Our company specializes in capturing the essential essence and scent of your favorite celebrities. Our researchers have found the perfect balance of each ingredient to ensure that every candle you buy holds the canon scent of your chosen icon.

Our Products

Products by Z's Candles are 100% cruelty-free, vegan, gluten free and are huge advocaters for recycling materials. Our high-quality candle wax is housed by recyclable mason jars. Once clients have burned our candles in their entirety, they are able to make use of the free mason jar that comes with it. We pride ourself on our contribution to an unpolluted earth and hope this inspires our customers as well.

Our Services
